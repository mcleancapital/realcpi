M2 is a way to measure how much money is in the economy. It includes:

- Cash and coins (money you have in your wallet or under your mattress) - Money in checking accounts (you can spend it right away) - Savings accounts (you can access it, but not as quickly) - Short-term deposits (like small certificates of deposit under $100,000)
